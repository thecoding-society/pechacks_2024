About

Why Hackathon (p)


"A hackathon provides a platform for students to come up with innovative ideas and develop creative solutions to real-world problems. In a hackathon, students can build technical skills such as coding, data analysis, and software development. These skills can help them in their future careers in the technology industry. "

Aim (p)

  • 1. Self Assessment
  • 2. Career Awareness
  • 3. Goal Setting
  • 4. Skill Training
  • 5. Performing

Career development is the series of activities in an on-going/lifelong process of developing one’s career which includes defining new goals regularly and acquiring skills to achieve them. Career development usually refers to managing one’s career in an intra-organizational or inter-organizational scenario. It involves training on new skills, moving to higher job responsibilities, making a career change within the same organization, moving to a different organization or starting one’s own business.

Prize Pool of 1 Lakh+ rupees.


Domains

Domains

01

Blockchain & Web 3:

Problem Statement


1. Design a solution that leverages blockchain technology to ensure transparent and accountable distribution of humanitarian aid, allowing donors to track their contributions and ensuring that resources reach the intended beneficiaries efficiently.
2. Hybrid-Healthcare: Build a blockchain-based system for securely storing and managing electronic health records, ensuring privacy, data integrity, and interoperability between healthcare providers.

02

Artificial Intelligence:

Problem Statement


1. Design an algorithmic trading system that leverages real-time data and machine learning techniques to make informed investment decisions.
2. Hybrid-Social Impact: Design an AI system that can detect and mitigate online harassment, hate speech, or fake news, promoting a safer and more inclusive online environment.

03

Healthcare:

Problem Statement


1. The healthcare industry lacks a unified platform for professionals to connect, collaborate, and share knowledge. This hampers effective communication, learning, and the ability to create comprehensive notes. A dedicated platform is needed to bring healthcare professionals together, fostering collaboration, improving understanding, and enhancing diagnosis and treatment practices.
2. Hybrid-AI: Develop a system for predictive analytics and resource optimization in hospitals, utilizing AI algorithms to forecast patient demand, optimize staffing levels, and allocate resources efficiently.

04

Social Impact:

Problem Statement


1. Build a platform that encourages civic participation and engagement among communities, facilitating communication between citizens and local government, promoting transparency, or enabling collaborative decision-making processes.
2. Build a platform that connects food surplus from restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals with local food banks or charitable organizations, reducing food waste and addressing food insecurity in communities.

05

Media and Content Creation:

Problem Statement


1. Develop a platform or tool that utilizes natural language processing to automatically generate captions, subtitles, or transcripts for audio and video content, enhancing accessibility and user experience.
2. Design a platform for content creators to easily manage and distribute their multimedia content across multiple social media platforms, streamlining the content creation and publishing process.
