FAQs

FAQs

Is this Hackathon an online event or offline?


This is an offline event.

Is there any registeration fee?


No, registrations are completely free of cost.

Can I participate without a team?


No, this hackathon is an team event and can participate in teams of 3 or 4.

Should you stay on campus overnight?


Yes, we request all hackers to stay on-campus during the entire hackthon to get the optimal experience.

What are the facilities arranged by the organizers?


Food, accomodation and awesome swags :) are arranged by us.

Can members from different college and branches form a team?


Yes, absolutely we encourage you guys to be diverse and join teams from various backgrounds and collaborate.

What I should bring for the hackathon?


Bring your Student ID, Laptops, Gadgets or anything else you want to work on.

Will the hackathon has Wi-fi facilities?


Yes absolutely, we'll provide Wi-fi Internet facilities.
